What is SEDA?

SEDA is a project for enhancing human learning by using state of the art techniques from computer vision, machine learning, natural language processing, and augmented reality. The non-technically constrained goal is to create an overlay to human vision to help with tasks humans are inherently bad at such as memory, calculations, and abstractions and to help speed up tasks such as looking up information and referencing material.
